About Me

Biography

I am a network engineering and cybersecurity specialist completing my final year of a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Uganda Martyrs University. My focus is on designing resilient network infrastructures and defending them from configuring routers,switches, firewalls, and VPNs to conducting vulnerability assessments and hardening systems against modern threats. I pair this technical depth with hands-on experience in server administration and IT support, giving me a full view of how networks are built, secured, and kept running in the real world.

My Career Focus

My objective is to become a trusted network and security engineer who keeps critical infrastructure resilient against evolving threats. I'm building expertise in network design, firewall and VPN configuration, and vulnerability management, backed by continuous hands-on lab practice and certification-track learning. Alongside this technical foundation, I maintain working knowledge of secure web development,enough to understand how applications and networks intersect from a security standpoint. My goal is to contribute to organizations that need dependable network architecture and proactive security defense.
